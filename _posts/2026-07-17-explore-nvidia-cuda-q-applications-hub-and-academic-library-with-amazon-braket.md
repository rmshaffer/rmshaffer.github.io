Amazon Braket now offers a straightforward way to explore NVIDIA CUDA-Q Applications Hub and Academic Library through a new set of example notebooks and workflows. These resources make it easier for researchers and developers to experiment with GPU-accelerated quantum computing workflows and to connect CUDA-Q-based applications with Amazon Braket's cloud quantum hardware and simulators.
